Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about dental treatments and our clinic.

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend visiting the dentist every six months for a routine check-up and cleaning. However, some patients with specific conditions may need to visit more frequently as recommended by their dentist.

Are dental X-rays safe?

Yes, digital X-rays use very low radiation levels and are completely safe when performed under professional supervision.

What is the right age for braces?

Braces can be done at almost any age, but the ideal time is between 10–14 years when teeth and jaws are still developing.

How long do dental implants last?

With proper care, dental implants can last 15–20 years or even a lifetime.

Does teeth whitening damage enamel?

No, professional teeth whitening is safe and does not harm enamel when performed under a dentist’s supervision.
